About Me

Hi there! I'm Miguel Rodriguez, a Rendering Engineer with around ~8 years of experience in the games industry.

I studied Computer Science in my hometown in Spain. As I was wrapping up my time in college, I realized I wanted to specialize in game development, which led me to study C++, games and game engines on my spare time.

After finishing my degree, I took a master's degree in videogame development and from there I moved to the UK to finally become a professional gamedev.

I spent some years working at Ubisoft Reflections, firstly as a Junior and eventually as a Graphics Programmer. I worked on different AAA projects there, including Tom Clancy's The Division 2.

After that, I moved to Striking Distance Studios, where I had the chance to work along industry legends like Jorge Jimenez and Glen Schofield.

In there, I was involved in some groundbreaking work pushing visual realism in The Callisto Protocol and I became a Senior Rendering Engineer along the way.

Currently, I am part of PUBG Studios working on an unnanounced project.

Other than that, I'm very passionate about videogames, game development and computer graphics in general.
