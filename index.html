About Me

I'm a graphics programmer working in the videogame industry.

I studied Computer Science in my hometown in Spain. In the last years of university, I realized I wanted to specialize in game development, which led me to study C++, videogame programming and commercial game engines on my spare time.

After finishing my degree, I took a master's degree in videogame development and from there I moved to the UK to finally become a professional game programmer.

For the last 3+ years, I have been working at Ubisoft Reflections, first as a Junior Programmer and then as a Graphics Programmer on different AAA projects.

Passionate about videogames, game development and computer graphics.
